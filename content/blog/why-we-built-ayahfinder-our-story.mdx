The personal journey behind the world's leading Quran recognition app.

I still remember the exact moment. Ramadan 2022, 15th night, standing in taraweeh at my local mosque in East London. The imam was reciting a surah that touched something deep in my heart. I wanted to know what it was. I wanted to read it again, to understand it, to make it part of my life. But I had no way to find out. I asked three people after prayer. None knew. I tried searching online with the few words I remembered. Nothing. That frustration stayed with me. Not just for that night, but for weeks. I knew there had to be a better way. And if no one else was going to build it, I would.
