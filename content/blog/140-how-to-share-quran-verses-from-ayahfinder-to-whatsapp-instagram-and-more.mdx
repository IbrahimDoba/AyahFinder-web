## Share Sheet Integration

AyahFinder integrates deeply with your phone's native sharing capabilities. Tap the share button on any verse and your device's standard share sheet appears, offering every app installed on your phone as a destination. This universal approach means you are never limited to preset options.

The shared content intelligently adapts to each platform. Text-heavy apps like WhatsApp receive properly formatted Arabic with translation. Image-focused platforms like Instagram get visually designed cards. Email receives comprehensive formatting with all metadata included.

Quick share options remember your frequent destinations. If you regularly share verses with specific family members or groups, these appear as one-tap shortcuts, streamlining the sharing process for your most common use cases.

## WhatsApp Sharing Features

WhatsApp receives special attention given its popularity among Muslim communities. Shared verses appear as beautifully formatted messages with Arabic text, translation, and audio preview if available. Recipients can play the recitation directly within the chat.

Status sharing creates elegant story-style posts perfect for WhatsApp Status updates. These temporary shares reach all your contacts with minimal effort, spreading Quran reminders throughout your network. The vertical format is optimized for phone screens.

Group sharing includes special features for study circles and family groups. Share a verse and automatically suggest discussion questions based on its themes. Add custom notes explaining why this verse resonated with you, personalizing the shared content.

## Social Media Optimization

Instagram sharing creates visually stunning posts that respect platform aesthetics. Choose from various background designs, select appropriate filters, and position text for maximum visual impact while maintaining readability of the sacred content.

## Preserving Meaning When Sharing

Sharing Quran verses carries responsibility. AyahFinder includes optional context information with every share, helping recipients understand the circumstances in which the verse was revealed. This historical background prevents misinterpretation.

Translation choices matter when sharing across language communities. The app can include multiple translations side by side, or you can select the most appropriate version for your specific audience. This thoughtfulness ensures the message translates culturally as well as linguistically.

The tafsir toggle lets you include brief explanations with shared verses. For complex ayahs that require scholarly context, this additional information helps recipients grasp deeper meanings they might miss from translation alone.
